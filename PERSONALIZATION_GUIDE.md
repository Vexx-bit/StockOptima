## 💰 **Multi-Currency Support (Kenya Priority)**

### **Supported Currencies:**

- 🇰🇪 **KSh (Kenyan Shilling)** - DEFAULT
- 🇺🇸 $ (US Dollar)
- 🇪🇺 € (Euro)
- 🇬🇧 £ (British Pound)

### **How to Change Currency:**

**Method 1: Quick Change (Header)**

1. Look at the top-right of any page
2. Click the currency dropdown (shows current currency)
3. Select your preferred currency
4. Page will reload with new currency

**Method 2: User Preferences**

1. Click the user icon (top-right)
2. Select "Preferences"
3. Choose your currency
4. Click "Save Changes"

### **Currency Features:**

- ✅ **Kenya-Optimized** - KSh is the default currency
- ✅ **Automatic Formatting** - Proper number formatting for each region
- ✅ **Persistent** - Your choice is saved
- ✅ **Global** - Applies to all prices throughout the app

### **Examples:**

```
KES: KSh 7,234.50
USD: $7,234.50
EUR: €7,234.50
GBP: £7,234.50
```

## 🔔 **Smart Notifications**

### **Real-Time Alerts**

Get notified about important inventory events!

### **How to View Notifications:**

1. Look for the **bell icon** (top-right)
2. Notice the **red badge** showing unread count
3. Click the bell to see all notifications
4. Click any notification to view details

### **Types of Notifications:**

**Low Stock Alerts:**

- Products below threshold
- Urgent restocking needed
- Shows product name and current quantity

**Stock Updates:**

- Stock additions confirmed
- Stock removals logged
- Quantity changes tracked

**System Alerts:**

- Important system messages
- Updates and announcements

### **Notification Features:**

- ✅ **Badge Count** - See unread notifications at a glance
- ✅ **Timestamps** - Know when events occurred
- ✅ **Categorized** - Different types of alerts
- ✅ **Clickable** - View details quickly
